/ about us. /

Due to her extreme fondness for ice cream, she decided to launch a pre-order for her friends to taste her new and unusual ice cream recipe. It transpired that her friends adored it and pushed her to start her own little company. She began her business on a modest scale, continued to get chances to collaborate with major companies, and was able to boost her sales within days before opening her first shop. It was a trip on a roller coaster, and things were not always rainbows, but I realised that nothing is impossible if you apply your whole focus and energy.

This ice cream business was founded with the purpose of not only selling ice cream
but also promoting local farmers and goods. We aim to guarantee that the vast
majority of our products originate from Indonesia and are of the best quality.
In addition, none of our products include any artificial flavours or colourings.
On top of it, to make it unique, we also added several topping and
syrup to make your ice cream game more exciting, and our very
famous waffle cone is also made freshly everyday for the extra crunch.

Making it less-sweet is also a very important aspect for us since we know
that too much sugar does not have any benefit to our health, hence we
made it less-sugar and using the finest sugar to bring out the best flavors.

Now with around 23 stores operating around Indonesia, Malaysia, and
Singapore, we hope we could come and say hi to those countries that
we have not yet to open.
